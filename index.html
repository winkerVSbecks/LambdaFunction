Oracle

Oracle is an interactive sculpture by artist Victor Zwiers that reacts to the presence of its audience. The sculpture targets and follows the movements of the viewer, all the while projecting a video onto them, forcing the participant to be part of the artwork. I was responsible for the technical side of the project and built a system using processing, arduino and a kinect to achieve the desired effect.

#kinect #processing #arduino Victor Zwiers

unCloud

As a part of my internship at INTK studios, I designed the project identity and assisted in the development of unCloud. It is an application that enables anyone with a laptop to create an open wireless network and distribute their own information. Once it is launched, a passerby using a mobile internet device can connect to this open wireless network. It does not depend on a remote data centre and instead it can be run entirely from a laptop.

#python #js INTK

Homesick

Homesick was my thesis project at the Interactive/Media/Design department at the Dutch Royal Academy of Art. It's an installation which I built in order to overcome the loss of my childhood home in India, which had recently been sold, demolished and replaced by an apartment building. I also built a webGL based app to help me design and build the structure.

#fabric #metal Project Site

Tethered Motion

Tethered Motion is a giant wind-up toy that comes to life when participants turn a large metal crank. Once animated, the flat wooden surface mimics a bird in flight. The sculpture stemmed from the my desire to recreate a childlike fervour for toys and token objects.

It was presented at the 2012 Scotiabank Nuit Blanche, Toronto.

#wood #metal Scotiabank Nuit Blanche 2012
